Emergency & Trauma Care

General Medicine

We offer complete care for common illnesses, chronic diseases, and preventive health. Our general physicians focus on diagnosis.

Cardiology

Our cardiology department specializes in heart disease prevention and treatment. From ECG and echocardiography to angioplasty, we cover advanced heart care.

Neurology

We provide specialized care for brain, spinal cord, and nervous system disorders. Treatments include stroke management, epilepsy, Parkinson’s, and more.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic unit manages fractures, joint replacements, and spine disorders. We offer both surgical and non-surgical treatments for bones and muscles..

Pediatrics

Comprehensive healthcare for newborns, children, and adolescents. Services include vaccinations, growth monitoring, and illness treatment.

Gynecology & Maternity

Dedicated care for women’s health, pregnancy, and reproductive wellness. We provide prenatal, postnatal.

Surgery

Our surgical team performs both general and specialized procedures. Modern operation theaters ensure precision and safety during treatment.

Diagnostics & Laboratory

Comprehensive diagnostic services including blood tests and pathology. Equipped with advanced machines for accurate and timely results.
